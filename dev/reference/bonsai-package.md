# bonsai: Model Wrappers for Tree-Based Models

Bindings for additional tree-based model engines for use with the
'parsnip' package. Models include gradient boosted decision trees with
'LightGBM' (Ke et al, 2017.), conditional inference trees and
conditional random forests with 'partykit' (Hothorn and Zeileis, 2015.
and Hothorn et al, 2006.
[doi:10.1198/106186006X133933](https://doi.org/10.1198/106186006X133933)
), and accelerated oblique random forests with 'aorsf' (Jaeger et al,
2022
[doi:10.5281/zenodo.7116854](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.7116854) ).
